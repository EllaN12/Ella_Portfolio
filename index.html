My Story

Where Audit Rigor Meets
Data Science

My journey from IT Audit professional to Data Scientist wasn't a pivot — it was a natural evolution. Spending years inside companies like EY, Regeneron, and Bank of China, I was already doing the work: interrogating data, finding anomalies, quantifying risk, and translating findings into boardroom-ready narratives.

What I discovered is that audit instincts are data science superpowers. The same skepticism that uncovers a $6M vendor payment error is what surfaces model bias. The same rigor behind a zero-findings compliance audit is what ensures reproducible analysis.

Today I combine Python, machine learning, and causal inference with deep domain expertise in financial services and healthcare to build analytics from the ground up.

Based in New York, open to roles in data science, analytics engineering, and AI-first marketing measurement.
